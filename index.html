Certificate of compliance in accordance with regulation 7(1) of the Electrical Installations, 2009

The Certificate of Compliance is a crucial document issued for industrial, commercial, and photovoltaic (PV) solar installations. This certificate serves as an official validation that the installation adheres to all relevant safety standards, regulations, and industry best practices

Purpose:

To carry out installations in accordance, all Key elements meet the requirements of the Occupational Health and Safety Act of 1993, and regulations made there under.

Key Elements:

Verification of Standards

Installation adheres with regulation7(1) of the Electrical Installation Regulations, 2009

Inspections:

Inspection is done by certified & registered Installation electricians, as per requirement: a) electrical installation regulations9(2) (a) (new electrical installation) or b) electrical installation regulations9(2) (b) (existing installation) or c) electrical installation regulations9(2) (c) (new part to existing installation).

Test:

Test & test report done in accordance with SANS 10142-1, by qualified & certified competent Electrician.
